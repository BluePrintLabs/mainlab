Question: So I have my Blank Index.html file now what?
Answer: Now it's time to write out some basic HTML.. let's start simple examples of what can go inside your index file.
Q: Keep in mind you always need an opening and closing tag. This is identified by either < for open or > for close. The stuff inside of the two arrows define what type of tag it is.
A: <p>This is a paragraph tag. </p>
Here's some other examples:
<head>This is the header tag. Inportant website info will go here</head>
<body>This is the body tag, most of your websites content will go here</body>
<footer>This is how you identify the footer of your website</footer>
<b>This is a bold tag. It will bold anything inside of it. </b>
