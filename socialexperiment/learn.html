Question: What is www.Social Experiment.info?
Answer: We're an Open Source Project. We generate code and project structure and publish it online for anyone to use. This practice of Open Source is common in Technology and Science
Q: What is Open Source and why would anyone do it?
A: It's the best way to speed up scientific discovery. When someone publishes their research under an Open Source License others can utililze it in their own ways thus adding to the overall research being done on that topic/issue.

Last but most importanlty is our Learning Center. For those new to code or technology it a great place to colloborate, learn, and contribute to the open source community.
